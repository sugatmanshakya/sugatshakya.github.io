About Me

I am Sugat Man Shakya, an academic and researcher with a strong background in both Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and agricultural sciences. Currently affiliated with London Metropolitan University, my work bridges these fields to explore innovative solutions and drive interdisciplinary advancements.

My expertise encompasses key areas such as Computer Networking, Java Programming, Computer Networks Security, and Cloud Computing. I am particularly interested in how these technological domains can be leveraged to enhance efficiency, security, and sustainability, especially within the context of agricultural research and development. Through rigorous academic inquiry and practical application, I aim to contribute to meaningful progress and knowledge dissemination.
